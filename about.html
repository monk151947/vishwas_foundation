Vishwas Foundation

Vishwas Foundation for the Physically Challenged is a non-governmental organization that was founded in 2020, with the aim of empowering the physically challenged individuals in India.

Our organization strives to improve the physical, mental, economic, social and spiritual well-being of the physically challenged population. According to the census conducted in 2011, 2.21% of the Indian population is physically challenged and they face numerous challenges in society.

The physically challenged individuals in India struggle to achieve economic independence due to limited job opportunities and difficulties in finding employment.

We believe that providing skill development and vocational training can aid in bridging this gap and help in achieving their economic independence. Additionally, we provide support to enhance their physical, social and mental well-being by organizing various Para sports training, events and workshops.
